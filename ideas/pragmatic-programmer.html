The Pragmatic Programer

The Pragmatic Programmer by Andy Hunt and Dave thomas is one of the most famous books on how to be a better software engineer. The book Discusses principles such as the MVC pattern and orthagonality. The book also discussed the characteristics of what they call "The Pragmatic Programmer". The book is divided into 8 chapters, 48 section and the appendices, preface and index is about 100 pages and the main content is about 260 pages.

I will try to include an explanation of each tip when I get time.
